# Portfolio Note

Some projects in this portfolio involve sensitive, collaborative, or institution-linked work. To present them responsibly, I share only selected case studies, sanitized workflows, synthetic data schemas, aggregate results, and high-level documentation.

For projects involving health data, institutional datasets, company records, or shared research outputs, I intentionally exclude raw datasets, private notebooks, credentials, unpublished full manuscripts, and implementation details that should not be publicly redistributed.

This approach allows me to demonstrate my work in data cleaning, statistical modeling, forecasting, machine learning, dashboard development, and analytical reporting while respecting data privacy, research ethics, institutional boundaries, and collaborative ownership.

The materials in this repository are intended for portfolio review, recruitment, and professional evaluation. They are not meant to serve as a full source-code release, dataset archive, or replication package unless explicitly stated.
